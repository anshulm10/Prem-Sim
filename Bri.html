No Room For Racism !

Brighton & Hove Albion Football Club

Brighton, is an English professional football club based in the city of Brighton and Hove. Founded in 1901, and nicknamed the "Seagulls" or "Albion". Matches against fellow south coast outfits Southampton and Portsmouth are occasionally labelled as local derbies by the media, but most fans of either team do not consider the other to be their rivals due to the over 60 mi (97 km) distance between the clubs, and the already well-established rivalry between Southampton and Portsmouth.
