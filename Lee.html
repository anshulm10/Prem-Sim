Leeds United Football Club

Leeds United Football Club is a professional football club based in Leeds, West Yorkshire in England. Leeds' main rivals are widely considered to be Manchester United. Before each home game, the team enters the field to "Marching on Together", which is also sung during games. Alex Ferguson has said that Elland Road has one of the most intimidating atmospheres in European football. Leeds also hold extreme bitterness towards the Turkish club Galatasaray following the deaths of the two supporters the night before a UEFA Cup semi-final in Istanbul in April 2000.
