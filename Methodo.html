Method :

Calculating Home Attack and defence strength
Attack Formula :- Home Attack Strength= hg/hx
(hg= Average Home Goals of a specific team)
(hx= Average Home Goals scored in the league)

Defence Formula :- Home Defense Strength= hxc/hc
(hxc= Average Home Goals conceded in the league)
(hc= Average Home Goals conceded by a specific team)

Calculating Away Attack and Defence Strength
Away Attack Strength= ag/ax
(ag= Average Away Goals of a specific team)
(ax= Average Away Goals scored in the league)

Away Defence Strength= axc/ac
(axc= Average Away Goals conceded in the league)
(ac= Average Away Goals conceded by a specific team)

Final Score calculation
Home Lambda=hx + ha - ad
[ hx= Average Home league goals scored]
[ha= Home Team Attack Strength(Home)]
[ad= Away Team Defence Strength(Away)]

Away Lambda = ax + as - hd
[ ax= Average Away league goals scored]
[hd= Home Team Defence Strength(Home)]
[as= Away Team Attack Strength(Away)]
